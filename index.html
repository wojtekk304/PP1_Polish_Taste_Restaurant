About Us

Who we are ?

Our restaurant is a cosy and magical place, worth visiting, full of copper pots and jars filled with goodies from our pantry. Simplicity is hidden in every corner.

What food do we cook ?

What do we cook? For a start, we will welcome you with our fresh homemade bread with lard made with cracklings and onion. Visiting us you will taste such delicacies as hand-made and home-made dumplings, juicy marinated ribs, hand-tarte golden potato pancakes, browned pork chops, polish. There will also be cookies for dessert.
